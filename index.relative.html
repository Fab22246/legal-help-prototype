Legal help for common life events

Use this service to check what legal step may apply, prepare some legal documents, and find out what to do next.

You can use it to:

  • prepare a simple will, if your situation is straightforward
  • plan what should happen to your money and property
  • check what may be needed after someone dies
  • understand how to sign, witness, certify or submit documents
  • change a name or update a record
  • prepare questions and information before speaking to a lawyer

This service gives general legal information. It does not give legal advice.

Start now

What this service cannot do

This service cannot:

  • decide who should inherit
  • say if a will is valid
  • settle a dispute
  • replace a lawyer
  • file documents for you
  • guarantee that a document will be accepted

When you cannot use this service

Do not use this service as your only source of help if:

  • people disagree about a will, estate, money, property or document
  • someone is pressuring you to sign or prepare a document
  • there is already a court matter about the issue
  • you need urgent help because of a deadline
  • your issue is not one of the topics listed on this page

You can still read the general information.

But you should speak to a lawyer or contact the relevant office before taking action.

Before you start

You may need:

  • your full name and contact details
  • details about the issue you need help with
  • any forms, letters or documents you already have
  • details of money, property or other assets
  • documents that show your relationship to another person

You can still continue if you do not have everything.

Prototype note

This is a prototype.

It does not create documents, file applications, take payments, book appointments or save your information yet.
