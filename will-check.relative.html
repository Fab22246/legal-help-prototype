Check if a simple will may be right for you

A will says what should happen to your money, property and belongings after you die.

It can also name the person who should manage your estate. This person is called an executor.

This check helps you see if the simple will route may be suitable.

This service gives general information. It does not give legal advice. It also does not say that a will is valid.
