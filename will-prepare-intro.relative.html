Prepare information for a simple will

Now answer some questions about what you may want a simple will to say.

This prototype will help you organise information about:

  • you
  • your executor
  • your money, property and belongings
  • the people you want to receive things in the will

This prototype does not create a will. It also does not say that a will is valid.

To make a will, you will still need to prepare the will document and sign it correctly with witnesses.

You may also want to store the signed will safely. The Records Branch is part of the Barbados court system. It provides a free safekeeping service for original signed wills. This means the original will can be stored safely after it has been signed and witnessed. This is different from filing a court application.
