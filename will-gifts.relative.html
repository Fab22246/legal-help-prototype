Specific gifts

Use this page to list any specific personal items or amounts of money you want to leave to named people.

Do not use this page for:

  • land or a house
  • a business or part of a business
  • property outside Barbados
  • anything owned with someone else

If you want the will to deal with those things, you should speak to a lawyer.
